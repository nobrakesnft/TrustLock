Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

1. What We Collect

DealPact collects the minimum data necessary to operate the escrow service:

DataPurposeSource
Telegram user IDIdentify users, link dealsTelegram API
Telegram usernameDisplay in deals, buyer/seller matchingTelegram API
Wallet addressOn-chain escrow transactionsUser-provided
Deal dataAmount, description, status, timestampsUser-created
Dispute evidenceText and photos submitted during disputesUser-submitted
Transaction hashesLink on-chain transactions to dealsBlockchain

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not collect your private keys or wallet seed phrases
  • We do not track your browsing activity or use cookies on our website
  • We do not collect IP addresses

3. How We Store Data

  • User and deal data is stored in a Supabase database with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled
  • Only the DealPact bot (using a service role key) can read and write data
  • Database access is restricted and not publicly accessible

4. Who Can See Your Data

  • Other deal participants: Your username and wallet address are visible to the other party in a deal
  • Admins/Moderators: Can view deal details and evidence when resolving disputes
  • Public blockchain: Wallet addresses, transaction amounts, and smart contract interactions are publicly visible on-chain. This is inherent to blockchain technology.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Telegram: DealPact operates through Telegram's Bot API. Your interactions are subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Database hosting. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Base blockchain: On-chain data is public and permanent.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any other third parties.

6. Data Retention

  • User records are kept as long as your account is active
  • Deal records are kept indefinitely for audit and dispute reference
  • On-chain data is permanent and cannot be deleted (this is a property of blockchain)

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • View your registered wallet using the /wallet command
  • Update your wallet address at any time
  • Request deletion of your off-chain data by contacting us (note: on-chain data cannot be deleted)
  • Stop using the service at any time

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including database access controls, environment variable management for secrets, and regular security reviews. However, no system is 100% secure.

9. Children

DealPact is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions about your data or this policy? Contact us on Telegram: @nobrakesnft
